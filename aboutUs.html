TOP NEWS is a public news channel in Kosovo. It is owned by Television of Kosovo (RTK; Albanian: Radio Televizioni i Kosovës) from the year 2012. It main purpose it to serve to its loyal readers the most recent and unbiased news regarding politics, economics, education, sports, showbiz and so on. It was first founded in the late 2000’s and it is going very strong. The TOP NEWS along with its awesome staff work every day in order to produce worth-reading articles, which are updated every day. It is a channel meant to serve the truth to the people and certainly NOT to spoil and encourage drama and unwanted conflict between celebrities, for which this channel is not about. It is located in the capital city of Kosovo, Prishtina Street Eqrem Çabej, 115 Bulevardi Bill Klinton, Prishtinë 10000.When first was established in the late 2000’s, along with their original editors, it began as a newspaper and was considered the official newspaper of Kosovo. But it faced so many hardships; its writers always being chased and threated and the working conditions being not ideal. But what kept this going was the impact that we were having at the people. With the advancement of technology after the year 2015 this channel went online. It was a bold and risky to say the least, move because it was a new concept and not everyone were accepting this because the low quality of computers which could harm the eyes if it was stared for too long at the monitor was considered an issue.
